KEY FEATURES

LICENSE

Driving license is an official document that permits an individual to operate or drive a motorized vehicle on road. It includes all types of vehicles starting from a moped to a truck. However, in order to facilitate the learning process, the RTO’s or the Regional transport offices issue a learner’s license first.

RTO EXAM

Preliminary test is held for applicants who apply for learner’s license. The purpose of the preliminary test is to check the basic knowledge of the applicants regarding traffic regulations, road signs, rules of driving a vehicle, duties of a driver, essential documents, etc. Multiple choice questions are asked in the test.

VEHICLE HEALTH

In order to check the fitness certificate of your vehicle on the internet, you will have to visit the online portal. Enter your vehicle's enlistment number, full case number and motor number, after which you can see all the vehicle details including the fitness certificate.
